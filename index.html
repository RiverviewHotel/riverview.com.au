The View

The best seat
in Murwillumbah

Sitting right on the banks of the Tweed River, the Riverview has one of the most coveted spots in the region. Watch the river drift by from the outdoor deck, or settle into the open-fronted dining room with a cold one and a view that never gets old.

Whether it's a weekday knock-off or a lazy Sunday long lunch — this is where Murwillumbah comes to unwind.

The Pub

Murwillumbah's
local on the river

The Riverview's been a fixture of Murwillumbah for generations. A proper Aussie pub with hardwood floors, cold taps, big screens and a riverfront deck that's hard to leave once you're settled in.

Whether you're stopping in for a cold one after work, bringing the family for a bistro dinner, or watching the footy on the big screen — everyone's welcome here.

The View & Entertainment

More than
just a pub

The deck sits right on the Tweed — timber benches, palm trees and a river view that makes the second round an easy decision.
